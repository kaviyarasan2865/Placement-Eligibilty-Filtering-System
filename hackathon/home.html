Department of MCA

The Master of Computer Applications(MCA) program is a postgraduate course designed to provide individuals with an in-depth understanding of computer science and its diverse applications. Through a balanced blend of theoretical knowledge and hands-on practical experience, students in the MCA program gain proficiency in programming languages, software development, database management, computer networks, and more. With a focus on industry relevance, the MCA program equips graduates to excel as software developers, systems analysts, IT consultants, and other roles in the ever-evolving field of technology. This program is ideal for those who seek to advance their careers in the tech industry or contribute to innovative research and development.





Faculty

Our department is proud to have a team of experienced and knowledgeable faculty members who are experts in their respective fields. They are committed to delivering high-quality education and nurturing the talents of our students.





Courses

We offer a diverse range of courses that cover both foundational and advanced topics in computer science. Our curriculum is designed to equip students with the skills and knowledge needed for a successful career in the technology industry.





Placement

The placement cell is an integral part of our department and plays a crucial role in locating job opportunities for MCA students passing out from this department. The purpose of the cell is to enhance the employability of the students and to match the corporate expectations by providing them career guidance and training program, groom their confidence and developing their personalities via Soft Skills, Aptitude and Technical Training.
